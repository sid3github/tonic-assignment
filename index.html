About Arogya

Arogya Finance and
how it works to help
the people throughout
the country

Arogya Finance, co-founded by Mr. Jose Peter and Mr. Dheeraj Batra,
is a social health care venture, which offers medical loans to the traditionally
un-bankable, using innovative risk assessment tools that allows them to
finance people outside the formal banking system. Patients borrow
from and repay Arogya Finance directly leaving them free to get treated wherever
they choose to do so.

Arogya Products

Standard Loan

Standard Loan is for your immediate medical emergencies be it any type of treatments or surgeries.

Pre-Approved Loan

Pre-approved Loan is for your future medical emergencies. A medical loan of upto Rs 5 lakhs can be pre-approved & treatment can be availed at any hospital, upto 75% of the medical bill can be covered.
